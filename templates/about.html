Athena is a free, open source Headache diary written for firefox OS. It's crafted on the criteria of the International Classification of Headache Disorders (ICHD-3 beta, 2013) to discriminate between migraine and tension-type headaches. It's meant to assist medical anamnesis and to make things easier to both physicians and patients.
